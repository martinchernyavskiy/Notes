*Suppose $T:V \to W$ is linear.
Null Space (Kernel) of $T$ is:
$N(T)=\{ v \in V|T(v) = \vec{0_{W}} \} \subset V$
Range (Image) of $T$ is:
$R(T) = \{ T(v) | v \in V \} \subset W$*
